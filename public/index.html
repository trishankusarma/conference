Call for Papers

2021 IEEE SPARC-Conference on Microwave, Antennas and Propagation (SPARC-MAP 2021), jointly organized by National Institute of Technology Silchar, India, Delhi Technical University, Delhi, India and Queenâ€™s University, Kingston Canada under the aegis of SPARC Scheme of Ministry of HRD, Govt. of India, will be held at National Institute of Technology Silchar, Assam, India during February 5-7, 2021. SPARC-MAP 2021 is expected to bring together research scholars, academicians, industry professionals from reputed organizations to discuss, share and promote their recent achievements in the field of microwaves, antennas and radio-wave propagation and allied domains.

Suggested Research Domains(but not limited)

Track-1: Microwave Components, Circuits and Systems
Track-2: Antenna Engineering for RF Energy Harvesting/Wireless Power Transfer/Other Applications
Track-3: Radio Wave Propagation and Channel Modeling
Track-4: Communication, Signal Processing and Microelectronics Engineering for High Frequency Applications
Track-5: Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning Applications in Electronics and Communication Engineering

THREE

Young Scientist Programs(YSP)

Based on the significance of the work, novelty and technical contents, papers will be selected for the following awards: Best Student Paper Award (BSPA) and Best Conference Paper Award (BCPA) and Young Scientist Paper Award (YSPA). For details please see www.sparc-map2021.com.

Special Sessions/Tutorials/Workshops

Requests to organize special sessions/tutorials/workshops should be submitted to sparc-map2021@gmail.com no later than January 5, 2021. Each proposal should include the title of the special session, a brief description of the topic, and justification for its designation as a special session. All proposals should be submitted in PDF format. Special sessions will be selected and finalized by the end of January 2021.
